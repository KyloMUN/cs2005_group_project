Title: Review Result

Primary Actor:Student

Stakeholders and Interests:

Student - wants to review the result of a quiz

Precondition: User with Student privileges logged in already, user finish taken the quiz(all attempts completed or full mark was obtained).

Main Scenario:

  1. Student selects quiz for review
  2. SuD display quiz items from top to bottem follows the order: Student name and id, then all the attempts with its corresponding grade, lastly the highest grade
  3. Student selects one of the attempt as desired
  4. SuD displays the quiz questions with its corresponding correct answers and also with the student answers for student to review
  5. Student repeats step 3 to 4.

Extensions:

2a. No attempts shows if student did not take the quiz before the dead line.

2a.1 Student can select the view question to see the questions and answers of the quiz
